I like to build things. Here are some notable projects.

RE-volv.org

[repo]

A Django web app for RE-volv, a non-profit dedicated to crowd-funded solar energy projects. Developed pro bono with my Blueprint teammates, a UC Berkeley student organization that uses technology for social good.

αβ Pruning Practice

[repo]

A D3.js + Angular web app for visualizing and understanding the Alpha-Beta Pruning algorithm. Generates random trees, giving students unlimited practice problems that they can check against. Proved to be quite helpful for UC Berkeley’s CS61B.

Resistor Reader

[repo]

An Android application for working with resistor values. It’s simple, small, and user-friendly. This was my first mobile project, as well as my first Java project. The app is free on the Google Play Store.
